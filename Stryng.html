Class: Stryng

Stryng

new Stryng(value, is_mutable) → {Stryng}

Stryng's constructor behaves just like the native one does. static functions are only available on the Stryng namespace while instance methods are also available as static functions with inversed signatures i.e. they take the otherwise wrapped string as their first argument.
